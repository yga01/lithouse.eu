Only top-quality

21 Mar 2020
Translations: NL, FR
We only build top-quality. No quick fixes, no rush jobs, only solutions that will last. We prefer simple reliable technologies and stable solutions. We take our time to design, and we take our time to produce and assemble. We prefer things that are stable and don’t break. We want to build for ourselves, and for the next generations. It is not difficult, it’s just a matter of caring more about build-quality than about market share, because we think that comes automatically if you keep your eye on quality and customer satisfaction.

We build made-to-measure. No standard catalogue, only designs to your requirements. Takes a little extra effort, pays out for a lifetime. We build all over Europe

21 Mar 2020
Translations: NL, FR
We can build everywhere in Europe, but mainly in France and The Netherlands. We produce in Lithuania, our installation teams travel to your building site. Depending on the size of your project we show up with tree to six men teams, and we will take somewhere between five and twelve weeks to fully build your house. A log house normally takes slightly more time, but only slightly. Our main markets at the moment are France and The Netherlands, but we also build in Portugal, Belgium and Denmark.
